About USA

Who We Are

The UNIMA United Students Association (USA) is a student-led and student-run organisation founded by students of the University of Malawi. We are not a department of UNIMA — we are students who came together to create the community, support systems, and opportunities that we wanted to see on campus.

USA is our organisation. Our executive team is made up of your fellow students. Our programs are designed by students, driven by students, and built around what students actually need. Whether you are a first-year finding your feet or a final-year preparing for life beyond campus, USA is your home.

What We Stand For

  • Looking out for each other — promoting and protecting the welfare of every student at UNIMA
  • Helping each other succeed — peer-led academic support and tutoring programs
  • Growing together — leadership skills, entrepreneurship, and personal development
  • Giving back — community outreach, donations, and social impact initiatives beyond campus
  • Having fun — creating spaces for entertainment, socialising, and cultural exchange
  • Building bridges — partnerships with NGOs, institutions, and community organisations
  • Speaking up — advocating for student rights and transparent governance at UNIMA

What We Do

Activities & Programs

From academic support to community outreach, these are the programs our members organise, participate in, and benefit from together.

Motivational Talks

USA students visit primary and secondary schools across Malawi to inspire younger learners through powerful sessions on education, career planning, and leadership — because we remember what it felt like to need that encouragement.

Welfare Programs

Student-run initiatives supporting fellow members facing hardship — including peer counselling, emergency assistance, and support networks built on the principle that we look out for one another.

Donations & Outreach

Our members organise donation drives and community outreach programs to give back to schools, orphanages, and vulnerable communities — because we believe our responsibility extends beyond the campus gate.

Academic Tutorials

Students helping students. Our peer-led tutorial sessions and study groups mean that those who excel share what they know with those who need it most — because everyone deserves a fair shot at success.

Travelling Trips

Organised group trips that let our members explore Malawi and beyond together — building friendships, broadening perspectives, and creating shared memories that last long after graduation.

Movie Nights

Regular movie screenings organised by and for our members — a chance to unwind, laugh, and connect with fellow students in a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere.

Game Nights

Competitive and fun gaming events — board games, sports, and digital games — that bring students together for teamwork, laughter, and the kind of connection that only happens when you're not studying.

Dinner Events

Elegant dinners where we celebrate what our members have achieved, strengthen bonds within our student community, and mark the milestones that define our journey together.

Social Gatherings

Casual meet-ups, mixers, and social events where new members feel instantly welcome and existing members deepen the friendships that make university life truly meaningful.

Empowerment Campaigns

Leadership workshops, entrepreneurship sessions, mentorship, and gender empowerment campaigns — because we know the next generation of Malawian leaders is sitting in classrooms right now, and we want to help them get ready.

School Outreach

Our members partner with secondary schools to share guidance, mentorship, career advice, and real talk about university life — because we were in their shoes not long ago.

Community Service

Environmental clean-ups, tree planting, and community development projects run by our members — because being a student at UNIMA comes with the responsibility to make Malawi better.
